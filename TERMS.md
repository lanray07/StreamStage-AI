# StreamStage AI Terms of Use

Effective date: June 7, 2026

StreamStage AI is a private creator rehearsal simulator. By using the app, users agree to use simulated audience, chat, and viewer-count features only for private practice and preparation.

## Private Rehearsal Use

StreamStage AI must not be used to fake public engagement, mislead real audiences, impersonate real viewers, or deceive third-party platforms. Simulated chat, reactions, and viewer counts are practice-only features.

## Subscriptions

StreamStage AI may offer paid subscriptions through the App Store. Payments, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by Apple according to App Store terms.

## Content

Users are responsible for the content they rehearse, write, or save in the app. The app is intended to help with communication practice, sales rehearsal, Q&A handling, and creator confidence.

Features may change over time. Some AI, video replay, widget, Apple Watch, and backend functionality may initially be placeholder or mock-enabled while the app is developed.
