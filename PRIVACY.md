# StreamStage AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

StreamStage AI is a private creator rehearsal tool. It helps users practise live communication with simulated chat, simulated audience reactions, voice rehearsal, script drafting, and performance feedback.

## Data Collection

The current app is designed to work with mock/local AI features by default. StreamStage AI does not require account sign-in and does not sell user data.

The app may store rehearsal sessions, simulated chat, voice transcripts, script drafts, scores, and performance feedback locally on the user's device so the user can review progress. Local data can be deleted from the app settings.

## Voice and Microphone

StreamStage AI may request microphone and speech recognition permissions so users can practise speaking and view private rehearsal transcripts. Voice features are used for rehearsal and feedback.

## Purchases

If users buy a subscription, Apple processes the payment through the App Store. StreamStage AI may use StoreKit to check subscription status.

## Simulated Audience Data

Viewer counts, live chat, audience reactions, and pressure moments are simulated for private practice. They are not real public engagement and are not tools for deceiving audiences on third-party platforms.
